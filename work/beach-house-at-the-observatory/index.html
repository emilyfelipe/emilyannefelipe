Beach House at The Observatory

A poster series promoting Beach House's two recently-released albums and their show at The Observatory.

The two posters represent the dreamy landscape of the band's distinctive sound in Depression Cherry and Thank Your Lucky Stars, respectively. Both albums plunge into different musical terrain, and each poster visually expresses these subtle changes in mood and tone.
