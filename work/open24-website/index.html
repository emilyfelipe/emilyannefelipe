OPEN24 Website

We are 24 individuals with open minds.

This website was designed with this statement in mind as a promotional piece for the BFA Graphic Design Fall 2015 class at California State University, Long Beach, specifically for their senior show. It features key information about the event, the show’s mission statement, and links to the show’s Kickstarter and social media pages. The designers also are showcased on the page, and users can interact with them by hovering and clicking on the image. The website’s design and interface incorporates elements of the show’s branding, creating a cohesive look-and-feel that is connected to OPEN24 at its core.

(OPEN24 branding by John Crump, Mike Kwok, Kevin Parra, and Anne Peña. Photography by John Crump and Anne Peña. In collaboration with Amy Wong.)
