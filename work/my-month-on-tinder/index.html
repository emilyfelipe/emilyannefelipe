My Month on Tinder

A personal infographic detailing my journey through the world of online dating.

Tinder has redefined the rules of the dating game. A person is given a stack of options, and all it takes is a swipe to determine whether someone is a potential match or not. The first impression is all in a single photo, and once someone finds a match, there's the option to keep playing or to strike up a conversation. It's a simple process of elimination that is instant and based on instinct.

The gamification of online dating has increased our chances in meeting new people, but what is the end result? I decided to find out for myself by dedicating a full 30 days to the swipe life.
