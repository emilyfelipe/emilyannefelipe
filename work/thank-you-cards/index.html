Thank You Cards

It's never too late to say (and give) thanks.

A set of six cards that are designed with the recipient in mind. Although the cards encompass the same message and similar motifs, each variation possesses a certain uniqueness that distinguishes itself from the rest. Each card is carefully considered for a specific person, influencing the choice in certain colors and visual elements.

(Special thanks to Jonathan Kordich for taking photos of this project.)
